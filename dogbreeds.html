Dog Breeds Available

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a breed of dog that is known for its intelligence, loyalty, and friendly personality.

The Shih Tzu is a small breed of dog known for its long, flowing coat and affectionate personality.

The Siberian Husky is a breed of dog that is known for its thick, fluffy coat and energetic personality.

Pomeranian

The Pomeranian is a small breed of dog that is known for its fluffy, fox-like appearance and playful personality.

Doberman Pinscher

Bulldog

The Bulldog is a breed of dog that is known for its wrinkled face and friendly, calm demeanor.

Beagle

Rottweiler

The Rottweiler is a breed of dog that is often used for protection and is known for its loyalty and strength.

Dachshund

The Dachshund is a small breed of dog that is known for its long, sausage-shaped body and playful personality.

Chihuahua

The Chihuahua is a small breed of dog that is often carried around as a purse dog and is known for its bold personality.
