Feeding Tips

  • Choose a high-quality pet food that is appropriate for your pet's age, breed, and activity level.
  • Read the label and follow the feeding instructions carefully.
  • Establish a feeding schedule and stick to it.
  • Provide plenty of fresh, clean water at all times.
  • Avoid feeding your pet table scraps and human food.
  • Treats should make up no more than 10% of your pet's daily caloric intake.
  • Watch your pet's weight and adjust their feeding accordingly.
  • Monitor your pet's eating habits and behavior.
  • Make sure your pet's food and water bowls are clean and free from bacteria.
  • Consider feeding your pet a mix of wet and dry food for variety.
  • If your pet has special dietary needs or health issues, talk to your vet about the best feeding plan.
