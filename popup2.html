Exercise Tips

  • Provide daily exercise appropriate for your pet's breed, age, and health condition.
  • Engage in interactive play sessions with toys to keep your pet mentally stimulated.
  • Take your pet for regular walks or runs to maintain their physical fitness.
  • Consider activities like swimming or playing fetch to give your pet a full-body workout.
  • Provide opportunities for socialization with other pets or supervised playdates.
  • Use puzzle toys or treat-dispensing toys to keep your pet mentally engaged and physically active.
  • Monitor your pet's activity level and adjust the intensity and duration of exercise accordingly.
  • Consult with your veterinarian to determine the appropriate exercise routine for your pet.
